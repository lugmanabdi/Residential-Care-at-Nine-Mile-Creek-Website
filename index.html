About Us

Residential Care at Nine Mile Creek (RC-NMC), is a comprehensive home care provider serving individuals 18 years of age and over. RC-NMC focuses on providing a space where our population can age with dignity in an inclusive, caring home environment.

Our goal is to create a home where people can live comfortably and meaningfully-- and in a setting that honors their culture, religion, and identity.

RC-NMC provides private rooms, internet access, central air, onsite laundry, and numerous accessibility features. Additionally, we provide assisted living and medical services, and have a registered nurse avaliable at all times.

Come in for an initial intake to learn more about how we can support you or your loved one(s). A health care professional will conduct a health analysis and work with you on creating a care plan that fits you or your loved ones' needs.

We accept Minnesota Group Residential Housing payments and other subsidies for rent and food costs for qualified, low-income person(s). We also accept private pay/co-pay, long-term care insurance, and elderly waiver.

Our Services



Personal Care Assistance:



Essential help with activities of daily living (ADLs) such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and medication management, ensuring residents’ well-being and comfort.





Healthcare Services:



Access to on-site or on-call medical assistance, including help with managing chronic conditions, critical for maintaining residents' health and safety.





Nutritious Meals:



Regularly scheduled meals served with options for special diets or dietary restrictions, ensuring residents receive proper nutrition for their health and quality of life.
