Our Services and Accommodations

Accommodation

Private or semi-private rooms/apartments with bathrooms and basic furnishings, providing a comfortable living environment.

Nutritious Meals

Regularly scheduled meals served in a dining area, with options for special diets or dietary restrictions.

Housekeeping

Regular cleaning and maintenance services for residents' living spaces, ensuring a clean and comfortable environment.

Personal Care Assistance

Help with activities of daily living (ADLs) such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and medication management.

Healthcare Services

Access to on-site or on-call medical assistance, including assistance with managing chronic conditions.

Social and Recreational Activities

Organized events, outings, and activities to promote socialization, mental stimulation, and physical activity.

Transportation Services

Scheduled transportation for medical appointments, shopping, and other outings.

Security and Safety Features

Emergency call systems, secure entry, and staff trained in emergency procedures for residents' safety.

Fitness Facilities

Access to exercise equipment, fitness classes, or walking paths to promote physical well-being.

Beauty and Barber Services

Access to transportation to hair salons/barber shops for grooming and personal care needs.

Wi-Fi and Technology Access

High-speed Wi-Fi internet access for staying connected with loved ones and accessing entertainment. Each room includes a television for relaxation.

Cultural and Spiritual Support

Opportunities for residents to engage in religious or spiritual practices, with access to cultural events and celebrations.
